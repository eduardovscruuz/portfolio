ABOUT

About Me

Hi, I'm Eduardo, a passionate junior developer who discovered the magic of programming in the midst of a challenging time. I started studying in August 2022 and I've never looked back since.

For me, programming was a turning point that led me to discover my true calling in life. While studying at the law school, I always felt a sense of emptiness and lack of fulfillment. However, I chose to continue on that path as I didn't want to remain stagnant. It was during the last semesters that I discovered programming and immediately felt a connection to it. From a young age, I always had a knack for technology, and the ability to create something out of nothing, solve complex problems through it, is what drew me to this field.

Since then, I've been on a journey of constant learning and growth. I have dived deep into different techs and frameworks in general, building projects that showcase my skills and creativity. With each new challenge, I am fueled by a deep passion for problem-solving and the joy of creating something from scratch. As a part of your team, you can expect an individual who is not only hardworking and dedicated but also a fast learner and great team player. My commitment to my craft and ability to think outside the box makes me a valuable asset to any project.

In summary, I am a determined and enthusiastic junior developer who is excited to continue growing in my craft. With my newfound passion for programming and a drive to succeed, I am sure to make an impact in the tech world.
